Temple Activities

We have a vibrant community with many programs through the week

Bhagavad Gita Class

every Wednesday @ 7:00 PM at the temple. All are welcome.. For more information contact Temple President. Children's Spiritual Classes held at various places.

Community Outreach

Hari Naam every Friday at 6pm.

Krishna Fest

Join transcendental Krishna Fest. An hour & half of Transcendental Lecture, Bhajans (Indian Music & Dance) followed by sumptuous feast from the world's best and oldest traditional culture.

Bhakti Vriksha (Group Discussions)

Devotees gather to discuss Bhagavad Gita and other Transcendental topics in the association of other devotees to enrich their devotion.
